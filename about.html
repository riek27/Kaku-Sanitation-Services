About Kaku Sanitation

Leading the way in innovative sanitation solutions for South Sudan

8+ Years of Excellence in Sanitation Solutions

Kaku Sanitation Services South Sudan has been at the forefront of providing innovative sanitation solutions since 2015. Our commitment to safety, eco-friendliness, and long-term durability has made us the trusted choice for homes, businesses, and institutions across the region.

We specialize in advanced bioseptic tanks, grease traps, and water purification systems that are not only effective but also environmentally sustainable. Our solutions are designed to last for decades, providing reliable service with minimal maintenance.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering quality workmanship and exceptional customer service. We take pride in our attention to detail and our commitment to exceeding client expectations.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, eco-friendly sanitation solutions that improve public health and protect the environment in South Sudan.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of sustainable sanitation systems, transforming communities through clean and safe environmental practices.

Our Values

Integrity, innovation, sustainability, and commitment to excellence in all our services and customer relationships.
